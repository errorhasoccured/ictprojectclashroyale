Evolutions!

How to obtain them, what they give, and more.

What are they?

They are a new mechanic in the game allowing you to upgrade your cards in game.

How do you obtain them?

By obtaining evolution shards, which can be purchased through the season shop, special challenges, pass royale, shop offers, and more.

How do they work?

Whenever you play a card, that counts for a single cycle. Each evolution neds a certain number of cycles to be used. The evolution automatically applies, and after the evolved cards are played, the cycle restarts.

What buffs do evolutions give? How can this be used?

Different cards give different buffs, such as hit speed, damage, speed, hitpoints, and more. You can only have one evolution card in your deck. Since this is still a relatively new feature, we are still experimenting to see how the evolutions can be used to their full capabilities. Stay tuned!
