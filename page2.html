Level 15

Level 15 is the newest level introduced. It uses a new system of "elite" cards, which are unlocked after you get a card to level 14. But, level 15 is hated by the community. People have done calculations, and in leevling your card to level 14, you are only 1/3 of the way to level 15.

Level 15 requirements:

  • No gold!
  • "Elite cards"
  • A lot of grinding and patience
